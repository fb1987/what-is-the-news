MATRIX NEWS — ABOUT

What is this?

A WebGL homage to the classic Matrix “digital rain,” streaming real headlines in a dense, glyph-driven field. Hover any column to reveal the story; click to open the source. Pausing intensifies the CRT bloom for a freeze-frame poster look.

How it works

  • GPU grid render + per-cell glyph atlas
  • Trail-gated visibility (true “reveal” — no pre-lit text)
  • Staggered, per-char unscramble/scramble on hover
  • CRT post-FX: scanlines, halo bloom, chroma fringing, vignette, flicker
